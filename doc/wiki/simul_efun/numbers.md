## percent_of

## SYNOPSIS

    float percent_of(float a, float b)

### PARAMETERS

* `float a` - The part value.
* `float b` - The whole value.

## DESCRIPTION

Calculates what percentage `a` is of `b`.

## remainder

## SYNOPSIS

    varargs float remainder(mixed a, mixed b)

### PARAMETERS

* `mixed a` - The dividend.
* `mixed b` - The divisor.
